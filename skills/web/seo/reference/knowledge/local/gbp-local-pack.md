# The local pack: GBP as primary lever, ranked on relevance / distance / prominence

For a physical-presence business, the map "local pack" is ranked by Google on relevance, distance, and prominence, and the single biggest lever is a complete, verified Google Business Profile (GBP) — an off-site asset, so most local wins live outside the codebase.

## Facts
- Google states local results are "mainly based on relevance, distance, and popularity" (prominence). **Relevance** = how well the profile matches the query (fix: complete, detailed business info + correct primary category). **Distance** = proximity of the business to the searcher (largely uncontrollable). **Prominence** = how well-known the business is, informed by links to the business and by review count/rating. [support.google.com/business/answer/7091]
- Google is explicit: "There's no way to request or pay for a better local ranking," and "Businesses with complete and accurate info are more likely to show up." The lever is data completeness/accuracy on the GBP, not on-page tricks. [support.google.com/business/answer/7091]
- **GBP is off-site and cannot be edited in the repo** — like the entity/off-site ceiling in `answer-engines/entity-signals`. An audit surfaces GBP claim/completeness/category/hours/photos as *advisory*, never as a code fix.
- **Reviews feed prominence** and are a distinct operational lever: Google says "More reviews and positive ratings can help your business's local ranking," and responding to reviews helps you stand out. What matters is total count, average rating, **recency/velocity** (steady flow beats a stale burst), and response rate. [support.google.com/business/answer/7091]
- Vendor weighting (Whitespark/BrightLocal 2026, single-source, **confidence: low**): GBP signals ≈32% of local-pack influence, reviews ≈16–20% (rising YoY), on-page website signals ≈15%, citations ≈7%; primary GBP category cited as the most influential single factor. Directional only — do not quote as fact. [brightlocal.com/learn/google-local-algorithm-and-ranking-factors]
